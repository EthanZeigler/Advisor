PAWS Schedule Generator

THIS APPLICATION IS IN BETA. EXPECT WEIRD STUFF. I wanted to make sure it was out for registration


PAWS is missing tons of things that should be standard in a course management, but by far the worst is trying to figure out how to organize classes so they don't conflict. PAWS will only tell you if an arrangement is okay or not, but what it really needs is to tell you what arrangements are possible. Advisor takes your PAWS shopping cart and tell you what classes will go together.

How to use

Windows: press CONTROL+A (highlighting the entire page) then CONTROL+C (to copy it). Right click on the text box below and choose paste

macOS: press ⌘+A (highlighting the entire page) then ⌘+C (to copy it). Paste into the text box below.
